<img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1054" alt="ccral-wall" src="http://citycampnc.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/ccral-wall-300x225.jpg" width="300" height="225" />CityCamp NC 2013 culminates this afternoon with presentations by teams that formed during yesterday&#8217;s unconference. Six teams will be presenting on a variety of topics ranging from Yelp-like presentation of city building permits, to making a more-natural search interface for Wake County Library&#8217;s book catalog.

The public is invited to watch the presentations as they&#8217;re delivered in the auditorium of the amazing James Hunt Jr. Library on NCSU&#8217;s Centennial Campus. Presentations will begin at 4:00pm.

The six teams presenting are:<!--more-->

  * Utilities application
  * PulsePoint in the Triangle
  * GEO*Build
  * Local library
  * Let&#8217;s Meet NC
  * Education connectors
  * Crowdfunding legislation (not a competitor)
